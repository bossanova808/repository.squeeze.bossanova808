Enter what you want displayed on your Squeezebox lines during playback.

Note if multiple players are operating (e.g. you have music on during a slideshow), you will only get info from the highest priority player. The order of priority is Video, Picture, Audio.

The following tokens will be replaced by values (if available, otherwise null strings): 


		my %tokens  = 	
				(   "[current_date]", "",
					"[current_time]", "",
					"[duration]", "",		#presented in HH:MM:SS
					"[totaltime]", "",		#same as duration
					"[time]", "",			#presented in HH:MM:SS
					"[time_remaining]", "",	#calculated from duration - total time
					"[percentage]", "",		
					"[title]", "",
					"[album]", "",
					"[artist]", "",
					"[season]", "", 
					"[episode]", "",
					"[showtitle]", "",
					"[tvshowid]", "",
					"[thumbnail]", "",
					"[file]", "",
					"[fanart]", "",
					"[streamdetails_audio_channels]", "",
					"[streamdetails_audio_codec]", "",
					"[streamdetails_audio_language]", "",
					"[streamdetails_subtile]", "",
					"[streamdetails_video_aspect]", "",
					"[streamdetails_video_codec]", "",
					"[streamdetails_video_height]", "",
					"[streamdetails_video_width]", "",
					"[streamdetails_video_stereomode]", "",
					"[type]", "",
					"[resume]", ""
				);
